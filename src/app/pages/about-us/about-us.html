About Echoed

Your voice in music.

Our Mission

Echoed is a space to share what you’re listening to, discover new sounds through others, and connect over the stories behind songs and albums.

Every review, comment, and reaction builds a community where music feels more personal — and more alive.

Academic Origin

Echoed was created by students from the Universidad Tecnológica Nacional – Facultad Regional Mar del Plata (UTN-FRMDP) as a final project.

It was designed and developed as a full-stack web app — Spring Boot for the backend and Angular 20 for the frontend — to show how a student team can turn their passion for music into a working social platform.
